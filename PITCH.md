# PushBack - 2 Minute Pitch Script

*Read naturally, not robotically. Pause where it feels right.*

---

**[0:00 - 0:20] THE HOOK**

Every year, millions of people sign contracts they don't understand. Immigrants taking their first job. Gig workers accepting terms they've never seen before. Renters who don't know their lease has a clause that waives their right to sue.

They don't have $300 an hour for a lawyer. Most of them don't even know they *can* negotiate.

**[0:20 - 0:45] WHAT WE BUILT**

PushBack is an AI-powered contract coach. You upload any contract, and in under 30 seconds, Claude Sonnet 4.5 breaks down every single clause with a severity rating: red for dangerous, amber for concerning, green for fair.

But we don't stop at analysis. You pick a risky clause, and you *practice* pushing back. The AI plays your counterparty, whether that's an HR manager, a landlord, or a client. It responds realistically, and after each exchange, you get real-time coaching on what worked and what to try next.

When you're ready, PushBack generates a personalized negotiation script: exact phrases to say, ask, and insist on.

**[0:45 - 1:10] WHO THIS IS FOR**

We built this for the people the legal system ignores. Immigrants navigating unfamiliar labor laws. Gig workers with take-it-or-leave-it contracts. Freelancers facing one-sided agreements. First-time employees who were never taught that negotiation is even an option.

The free Community tier gives everyone 7 full consultations a month, with unlimited sparring and scripts. No credit card. No catch. Because the people who need this most can't afford a paywall.

**[1:10 - 1:35] WHAT COULD GO WRONG (AND HOW WE HANDLE IT)**

The obvious risk is AI giving bad legal advice. We designed against that from day one.

First, PushBack is not a lawyer and never pretends to be. Every screen carries a legal disclaimer. The tool is framed as a *coaching and literacy tool*, not legal counsel.

Second, we force the AI to quote the exact text from the contract when explaining clauses. It can't hallucinate terms that aren't there. If the clause says 18 months non-compete, that's what gets analyzed.

Third, we have a human handoff design. When a clause is flagged as critically dangerous, we don't just advise. We surface local legal aid resources and encourage the user to consult a real attorney before signing. We help people understand their situation, then connect them to professionals for the decisions that matter most.

**[1:35 - 1:55] HOW THIS HELPS PEOPLE**

PushBack doesn't make decisions for anyone. It builds the confidence to make their own. After three rounds of sparring, a first-time employee knows what a reasonable non-compete looks like. A gig worker understands which clauses are standard and which ones aren't. A renter can walk into a lease signing and ask the right questions.

We're not replacing lawyers. We're giving people the knowledge to advocate for themselves. That's financial literacy. That's economic empowerment.

**[1:55 - 2:00] CLOSE**

The fine print has met its match. This is PushBack.
