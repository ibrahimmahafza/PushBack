Negotiation Sparring

"I'd like to discuss the non-compete clause. 18 months seems excessive for this role…"
🏢 HR Manager
"That's our standard agreement. It protects our client relationships and proprietary processes."
"I understand protecting relationships, but could we limit it to direct competitors within 50km? That still protects your interests."
🎯 Coaching: Great move — you acknowledged their concern, then proposed a specific alternative. This is much stronger than just saying "that's too long."
